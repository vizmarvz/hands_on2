OUTSOLE

Elastic polymers, such as ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA), and elastane, play essential roles in our products. An EVA footwear sole offers numerous advantages, as it is lightweight and flexible, making it an ideal choice for shoe bases.

RECYCABLE MATERIALS

Since 2024, we've replaced virgin polyester with recycled polyester wherever possible. And we're not stopping with polyester. We're also using recycled materials like rubber and EVA in our footwear and recycled polyamide for apparel.

PIGSKIN NUBUCK LEATHER

Top Grain Leather, whose surface has been buffed and brushed to create a soft, velvety effect. Differs from suede in that while suede is created from the flesh (inner) side of a hide, nubuck is created using the grain (outer) side, giving it added strength and durability.
